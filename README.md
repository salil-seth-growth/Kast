## What this is

Instead of a cover letter, I wrote a growth plan.

This document maps KAST's funnel, identifies the highest-leverage gaps, and 
proposes four concrete growth experiments — each with mechanics, instrumentation, 
and success metrics. It also includes a 4-tier affiliate program redesign and a 
90-day execution plan.

---

## The four experiments

| # | Experiment | Funnel Stage | Primary Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Proof-of-Spend Sharing** | Acquisition | Viral K-factor, funded account CVR |
| 2 | **Welcome Yield** | Activation | KYC → first deposit conversion |
| 3 | **Pay with Yield** | Retention | Monthly card spend per funded user, TVL |
| 4 | **HODL Calculator** | Retention + Acquisition | High-value depositor retention, lead-gen |

---

## 30 / 60 / 90 day plan

- **Days 1–30 — Diagnose & Ship Fast:** Walk the full funnel personally, own the 
  metrics dashboard, audit the affiliate program, ship Experiment #1, interview 
  20 users
- **Days 31–60 — Build & Validate:** Launch Welcome Yield A/B test, scope Pay with 
  Yield PRD, build regional KOL strategy, document the experimentation operating model
- **Days 61–90 — Scale & Compound:** 3–5 experiments running in parallel, Pay with 
  Yield live, 60-day growth review presented to leadership
