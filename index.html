Growth Experiments
4 high-leverage experiments — one per funnel stage

KAST's funnel is a "Store, Spend, Earn" loop — these experiments treat it as one connected system, not isolated features. Each targets the highest-leverage conversion gap at its stage.

01
Acquisition · Affiliate Scale
Proof-of-Spend Social Sharing — The Network Effect
"Every KAST transaction becomes a distribution moment."
A "Wrapped"-style social sharing mechanic for the KAST card. When a user hits a milestone — first spend, first $100, first international transaction — they get a shareable graphic auto-embedded with their referral link and QR code. Designed for X and Telegram, where your user base lives. The referrer earns a bonus when their share leads to a funded account — closing the viral loop.
Milestone Triggers
  • First card spend — the moment the product becomes real for a new user
  • First Premium card spend — higher-status share template, stronger social signal
  • Sizable cashback on a single transaction — "look what KAST just paid me back"
  • Sizable cashback milestone in a month — monthly reward summary as a shareable graphic
Target: 15–25% of actives share within first week Direct K-factor uplift for affiliate program
02
Activation · KYC Completion
Welcome Yield — The Sunk Cost KYC Motivator
"Make KYC feel like the start of earning, not a compliance gate."
KYC is where most FinTech products bleed users. The problem isn't the process — it's the psychological framing. Users give data with no immediate reward. The fix: grant a "Welcome Yield" of up to 10% APY on the user's first $1,000 deposit. The yield is visible immediately at signup — but only becomes claimable once KYC is approved. The user now has a financial reason to complete, not just a product reason. Simultaneously solves KYC completion and first-fund conversion in one mechanic.
Mechanics
  • At signup, show a live ticker: "Your Welcome Yield is accruing. Complete KYC to claim it."
  • Yield counter visible on app home screen during KYC pending — ticking up in real-time
  • Push notification at KYC drop-off points: "You have $X in unclaimed yield waiting"
  • Yield claimable only after first deposit — bridges KYC completion and first-fund in one step
Target: 20–30% KYC completion lift Reduces effective KYC CAC
03
Usage · Revenue · Spend Velocity
Pay with Yield — The Passive Spending Experience
"Your yield pays your bills. You never have to move money again."
A single toggle in the app: "Pay Card Balance with Yield." When enabled, KAST automatically skims the daily yield from the Earn account to offset card transactions in real-time. This collapses the mental friction of "Store vs Spend" — users stop thinking about moving money and start experiencing the card as a passive income engine that funds their lifestyle. This is the product moment that converts a crypto card into a primary bank replacement.
Mechanics
  • Toggle in settings: "Auto-pay card balance with daily yield" — on/off, no minimum threshold
  • Real-time notification: "Your $12.40 lunch was paid by your USDC yield today"
  • Monthly summary card: "Your yield covered $340 in card spending this month" — shareable
  • Fallback: if yield insufficient for a transaction, card draws from main balance seamlessly
Target: 25% increase in monthly card spend per funded user Earn TVL uplift
04
Retention · Mental Model Shift
HODL & Spend Engine — Fund Your Life with Your Portfolio
"Stop spending money. Start managing cash flow."
Instead of converting crypto to spend, users deposit ETH, earn ETH rewards, and fund daily expenses from that yield — without ever selling their underlying position. The HODL Calculator is the lead-gen engine: it uses the user's actual past monthly spending to compute exactly how much ETH or USDC they need to hold to cover their lifestyle entirely from yield. This makes KAST structurally irreplaceable — switching back to a traditional bank is a financial downgrade, not just a preference.
Mechanics
  • HODL Calculator: pull avg. monthly card spend → compute required deposit → show as a personal goal ("Hold $18,000 USDC to cover your lifestyle with zero principal spend")
  • Progressive milestones toward the HODL goal — gamified with badges and community visibility
  • Monthly "Cash Flow Report": yield earned vs expenses paid — positions KAST as wealth management, not just a card
  • B2B angle: target Web3-native businesses and crypto-payroll workers who receive stablecoins monthly
Target: 40% increase in avg. deposit size Primary bank replacement rate uplift
Priority build
Building the #1 affiliate program in Fintech & Web3 ecosystem

Your current program has the right structure. The likely gap: a high ratio of dormant affiliates who sign up and never convert their first referrals. I've solved this exact problem before — at Binance with the copy trading leaderboard, and at Boost with the loyalty tier architecture. Here's the playbook:

01 · Solve the Dormant Affiliate Problem
  • The "24-Hour Blitz" — Activation Engine: if an affiliate converts their first referral (card order + min spend) within 24 hours of joining, they receive a 2× point multiplier or a one-time $10 Activation Bonus. Targets the "zero-referral gap" where most affiliates lose interest on Day 1. Success metric: % of new affiliates who move from 0 → 1 referral within Day 1.
  • The "Success Pulse" — Operational Transparency: The Black Box effect discourages affiliates who see no progress until the final spend trigger. Fix: high-granularity push/email notifications for Micro-Wins at every step — someone clicked your referral link · someone completed KYC · someone started spending (but <$100) · referral success (reward unlocked).
  • Reactivate 15d+ dormant affiliates with a targeted re-launch boost campaign: content support + non-monetary incentives including reductions in Premium / Luxe card fees. For affiliates who are content creators, distribution beats dollars — offer to Spotlight the top reactivated affiliate of the week in the KAST newsletter or on the official KAST X account.
  • Top-100 affiliate community (Telegram) with early feature access + direct team feedback channel — turns top performers into product advocates, not just referrers.
Goal: Active affiliate rate from ~15% → 40%+ in 90 days
02 · Segment by Channel Type
  • Crypto KOLs: elevated base commissions, co-branded campaigns, early Premium card access for their audience — highest-leverage distributors in the existing KAST community.
  • High-inflation market community builders (LatAm, SEA, Nigeria): geo-specific bonus pools, local-language onboarding support — users here have existential motivation to switch to stablecoin banking.
  • B2B connectors — Web3 payroll platforms, DAOs, remote-work communities: white-label onboarding pages + volume SLAs for structured partner programmes.
  • Regional "Dominance" Pools — Global rewards don't account for purchasing power parity or local market trends. Launch Monthly Regional Sprint Pools: e.g. "Top 5 referrers in the Philippines this month share a $2,000 bonus pool." Creates localised mini-leaderboards that feel achievable to non-global influencers. Success metric: market share growth in targeted high-growth corridors (LatAm / SEA / Africa).
  • Content Creator Programme — an additional segment beyond pure referral. Top 5 most informative or creative pieces of KAST content each month are highlighted and showcased via official KAST channels. Rewards quality reach, not just conversion volume — and generates organic top-of-funnel content at no paid media cost.
Goal: 3× affiliate-driven funded accounts in 90 days · measurable geo-specific market share growth
Execution
What I'd actually do in the first 90 days
Days 1–30
Diagnose & Ship Fast
  • Walk the full funnel personally — signup → KYC → deposit → first card spend — multiple times on both platforms; feel every friction point
  • Own the funnel metrics dashboard: define drop-off by step, segment by source; identify top 3 conversion gaps with data
  • Audit the affiliate program: active vs dormant ratio, time-to-Level-2 analysis, fraud signal review, top cohort behaviour
  • Ship Experiment #1 (Proof-of-Spend sharing) — simple version, not perfect. Instrument tracking before launch.
  • Interview 10 users who funded and 10 who dropped off post-KYC — validate the Welcome Yield hypothesis with real drop-off reasons
Days 31–60
Build & Validate
  • Launch Welcome Yield experiment (Exp #2) — A/B test with/without the accruing yield counter during KYC pending state
  • "Pay with Yield" toggle (Exp #3): PRD written, engineering sprint scoped, design signed off this period
  • Formulate regional KOL content strategy and plan for further expansion with Regional "Dominance" Pools and Content Creator Programme as a structured experiment
  • Build and document growth experimentation operating model — hypothesis template, prioritisation framework, experiment log shared across product team
Days 61–90
Scale & Compound
  • 3–5 experiments running in parallel at all times — the cadence that drove 10× MAU at Binance
  • Pay with Yield live; instrumented for TVL impact and monthly card spend per funded user
  • HODL Calculator (Exp #4): PRD complete; positioned as lead-gen tool and retention anchor for high-value depositors
  • First 60-day growth review presented to leadership: what moved, what didn't, what we double down on
  • High-inflation market brief: LatAm and SEA user research complete; product requirements for geo-specific affiliate GTM written
